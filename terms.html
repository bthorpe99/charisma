Eligibility

Charisma is for adults age 18 or older. By creating an account or using Charisma, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, that the information you provide is truthful, and that you will follow these Terms and our safety rules.

Account and Profile

Users create an account with email and password. During sign-up, users must enter a date of birth for the required 18+ age check. Users under 18 are blocked from account creation. Users must verify their email before entering live matching. Users provide profile information such as age, location, language, gender identity, and dating preferences so matching can be limited to compatible preferences.

Live Video and Safety

Charisma offers live video matching through third-party video infrastructure. Camera and microphone media are used for private live video and are not recorded by Charisma. Users can end, report, or block a match if an interaction feels unsafe or violates policy.

Payments

Membership and Grace Point payments are processed by Stripe. Charisma does not store card numbers. Grace Points and membership access are connected to the verified account used to purchase them. Refunded digital benefits may be removed from the account.

Moderation

Charisma may remove users from matching, suspend accounts, permanently ban accounts, preserve records, or report conduct to law enforcement when required by law or when a serious safety issue is identified.

Beta Features

Charisma is improving during beta. Features, pricing, questions, matching rules, access limits, and digital gifts may change as we improve safety and reliability.
