Charisma Safety

Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Adult-Only Service

Charisma may be used only by adults age 18 or older for lawful, consensual dating and social connection. Users must enter a date of birth during account creation, and users under 18 are blocked from creating an account. Users must verify their email before live matching.

Prohibited Conduct

  • Human trafficking, sex trafficking, prostitution, sexual exploitation, grooming, coercion, or solicitation.
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), sexual content involving minors, exploitation of minors, or attempts to contact minors for sexual purposes.
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery, sexual content shared without consent, threats to share intimate imagery, or profiles created without a person's consent.
  • Harassment, threats, hate, scams, impersonation, stalking, extortion, fraud, illegal activity, or sale/promotion of regulated goods or services.
  • Aiding, advertising, arranging, encouraging, concealing, or facilitating abuse, exploitation, or unlawful behavior.

Reporting and Blocking

Users can report a policy-violating profile or interaction directly inside Charisma using the Report button during a match. Users can also block another user immediately. For issues outside the app or urgent safety concerns, users can contact Charisma at charismalivesite@gmail.com.

Review and Enforcement

Charisma reviews reported profiles, account details, match context, user history, and the specific policy concern. High-risk reports involving exploitation, trafficking, threats, minors, or immediate safety danger are prioritized. Depending on severity, Charisma may remove a user from matching, suspend an account, permanently ban an account, preserve relevant records, or escalate to law enforcement when legally required.

CSAM and Minor Safety

Any suspected CSAM or minor exploitation is treated as an urgent safety issue. Charisma removes the violating account from the platform, preserves relevant records where appropriate, and escalates to law enforcement or the appropriate child safety reporting authority when required by law.

Appeals

A removed or banned user may appeal by emailing charismalivesite@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Appeal" and the email address connected to their account. Charisma reviews the appeal, original report, account history, and any additional context. Serious safety violations, exploitation, trafficking, CSAM, threats, or repeat abuse may not be eligible for reinstatement.
